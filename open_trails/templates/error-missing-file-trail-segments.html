You are missing a trail_segments.geojson file.

Trail segments are a critical component of OpenTrails data. Trail segments represent the geometry and alignment of trails. They are grouped into Named Trails that provide visitors with an understanding of the location and extent of your trail system.

For more information, please review the Open Trail System Specification (OpenTrails) in detail.
