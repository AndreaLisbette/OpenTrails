Information About Your Organization

Each geographic feature in OpenTrails is associated with a "steward": the organization primarily responisble for handling visitor questions and requests for assistance. For each steward, OpenTrails requires the information solicited in this form. Remember, the data is intended for visitor use first and foremost

The only outlier is the data license. It's important for third-parties to know uses of your data are appropriate. If you don't have a publicly accessible license for your data, let us know. We also recommend reading Open Data Handbook's resources on open licenses.


Please note, the content of this form is not currently validated. Please take care to provide:
  • A valid URL
  • A valid phone number, with area code.
  • A valid mailing address
  • A valid URL to a data license.
